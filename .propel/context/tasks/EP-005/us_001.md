# US-001 — Timezone-Aware Interview Scheduling with Panelist Conflict Detection

## User Story

**As a** recruiter,  
**I want** to schedule interviews with automatic timezone conversion for all participants and real-time conflict detection that warns me if a panelist is already booked,  
**So that** scheduled interviews are accurate across geographies and panelist double-bookings are prevented.

## Business Value

Timezone errors and double-bookings are the most common causes of interview cancellations. Automated conflict detection at scheduling time prevents the candidate and panelist experience damage of last-minute reschedules.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: Interview created with correct time for all participants
**Given** a recruiter in UTC+0 schedules an interview at "10:00 AM IST (UTC+5:30)"  
**When** the interview is saved  
**Then** the database stores UTC time; the candidate's calendar invite shows 10:00 AM IST; the panelist's invite shows their local equivalent.

### Scenario 2: Conflict warning shown before confirming
**Given** panelist A already has an interview at 10:00 AM on Tuesday  
**When** the recruiter tries to schedule another interview for panelist A at 10:00 AM Tuesday  
**Then** a conflict warning modal shows "Panelist A is unavailable: [existing interview title] at [time]" and the "Confirm" button is disabled until a different slot or panelist is chosen.

### Scenario 3: Available slots shown from panelist calendar
**Given** panelist B has marked availability for Tuesday 9–11 AM  
**When** the recruiter opens the scheduling UI for panelist B  
**Then** only 9–11 AM Tuesday slots are offered as selectable options; booked slots are greyed out.

### Scenario 4: Interview creation emits calendar invites within 5 minutes
**Given** an interview is confirmed  
**When** the booking is saved  
**Then** calendar invites (.ics) are emailed to the candidate, all panelists, and the recruiter within 5 minutes.
