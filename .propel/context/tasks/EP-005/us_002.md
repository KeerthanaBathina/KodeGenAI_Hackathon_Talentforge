# US-002 — Panel Member Management — Assign, Availability, and Confirmation Tracking

## User Story

**As a** recruiter,  
**I want** to assign panel members to interviews, view their availability windows, and track whether each panelist has confirmed their attendance,  
**So that** every interview has a confirmed, available panel before the candidate is notified.

## Business Value

Unconfirmed panelists are a leading cause of last-minute interview cancellations. Tracking confirmation status enables the recruiter to follow up proactively and ensures the candidate experience is not disrupted.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: Panelist added with availability check
**Given** a recruiter opens the panelist assignment panel for an interview  
**When** they search for and select a panelist  
**Then** the system immediately shows whether the panelist is available for the proposed time slot (green tick or red conflict indicator).

### Scenario 2: Confirmation request sent to panelist
**Given** a panelist is assigned to an interview  
**When** the assignment is saved  
**Then** the panelist receives a confirmation request email with "Confirm" and "Decline" links that update their confirmation status via a signed token URL.

### Scenario 3: Interview blocked from candidate notification if panelist unconfirmed
**Given** one panelist has not confirmed  
**When** the recruiter tries to notify the candidate of the interview  
**Then** a warning displays "Panelist [Name] has not confirmed. Notify candidate anyway?" with a required override acknowledgement.

### Scenario 4: Confirmation status real-time update on recruiter panel
**Given** a panelist clicks "Confirm" in their email  
**When** the token is validated  
**Then** the recruiter's panelist list updates the panelist status to "Confirmed" in real time via WebSocket within 2 seconds.
