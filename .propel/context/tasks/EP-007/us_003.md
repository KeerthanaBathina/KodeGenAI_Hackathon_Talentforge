# US-003 — Multi-Tier Approval Chain Workflow with Email Notifications

## User Story

**As a** hiring manager,  
**I want** offer decisions requiring approval to route automatically through the correct multi-tier approver chain based on the compensation band, with email notifications at each step,  
**So that** offer letters are only generated after all required approvals are granted and no approval is silently bypassed.

## Business Value

Approval chain enforcement ensures compensation governance is respected and provides an auditable record of who approved what and when. Email-driven notifications keep approvers informed without requiring them to log into the platform.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: Correct approval chain determined by compensation band
**Given** an offer for a role with compensation band "L5" requires VP and CFO approval  
**When** the hiring manager submits an offer decision  
**Then** the approval task is created for VP first; CFO approval is queued but not triggered until VP approves.

### Scenario 2: First approver receives email and can approve in-platform
**Given** the approval task has been created for the VP  
**When** the system triggers the notification  
**Then** the VP receives an email with a direct "Approve" and "Reject" link; clicking "Approve" opens the platform approval confirmation.

### Scenario 3: Second-tier approval triggered after first-tier approval
**Given** the VP has approved  
**When** the first approval is recorded  
**Then** the CFO receives an approval request email within 2 minutes; the application status remains `"pending_approval"`.

### Scenario 4: Rejection at any tier terminates the chain
**Given** the VP rejects the offer  
**When** the rejection is recorded  
**Then** no further approvals are requested, the application status changes to `"offer_rejected"`, and the hiring manager is notified immediately.

### Scenario 5: Full approval unlocks offer generation
**Given** all required approvers have approved  
**When** the final approval is recorded  
**Then** the application status changes to `"approved_for_offer"` and the offer letter generation flow is triggered (EP-007 US-004).

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Approval chain queried from `approval_policies` by compensation band
- [ ] Sequential approval: each tier only notified after prior tier approves
- [ ] Approval request email with secure approve/reject links (signed token, 72h expiry)
- [ ] Rejection at any tier: chain terminated; hiring manager notified
- [ ] Full approval: status → `approved_for_offer`; offer generation triggered
- [ ] All approval events written to `audit_events` with approver ID and timestamp
