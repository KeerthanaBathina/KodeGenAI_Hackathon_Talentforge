# US-003 — GDPR PII Anonymisation Within 30 Days and 7-Year Audit Log Retention

## User Story

**As a** data protection officer,  
**I want** candidate PII (name, email, phone, address) anonymised within 30 days of a retention deletion request and audit logs retained for 7 years before archival,  
**So that** the platform complies with GDPR Right to Erasure obligations while satisfying statutory audit record retention requirements.

## Business Value

GDPR non-compliance carries fines of up to 4% of global annual turnover. Automated anonymisation prevents human error in erasure workflows. Configurable 7-year audit retention satisfies employment law and financial audit requirements simultaneously.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: PII anonymised within 30 days of erasure request
**Given** a candidate submits a GDPR erasure request  
**When** the scheduled anonymisation job runs (daily at midnight)  
**Then** all requests older than 0 days and within 30 days have `name → "ANONYMISED"`, `email → "anon-<uuid>@redacted"`, `phone → null`, `address → null` applied; the anonymisation timestamp is stored.

### Scenario 2: Audit events referencing anonymised candidate use anonymised values
**Given** a candidate's PII has been anonymised  
**When** the audit log is queried for events referencing that candidate  
**Then** the candidate fields in the audit payload show anonymised values; no raw PII is retrievable.

### Scenario 3: Audit logs older than 7 years are moved to cold storage
**Given** an audit event has `created_at` more than 7 years ago  
**When** the monthly archival job runs  
**Then** the event is exported to Supabase cold storage bucket, the `audit_events` row is deleted, and an archival record is created in `audit_archive_index`.

### Scenario 4: Anonymisation does not delete hiring outcome records
**Given** a candidate's PII is anonymised  
**When** the anonymisation completes  
**Then** application, screening, interview, and decision records remain intact with the anonymised candidate reference; only the PII fields on the `candidates` table are cleared.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Daily anonymisation cron job: processes pending erasure requests ≤ 30 days old
- [ ] Anonymised fields: `name`, `email`, `phone`, `address`, `date_of_birth`
- [ ] `candidates.anonymised_at` timestamp written on completion
- [ ] Application, screening, interview, and decision records preserved with anonymised reference
- [ ] Monthly archival job: exports `audit_events` older than 7 years to cold storage; deletes rows
- [ ] `audit_archive_index` table tracks archived batches with date range and storage path
