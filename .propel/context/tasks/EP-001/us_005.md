# US-005 — Candidate Profile CRUD with Onboarding Checklist and Consent Management

## User Story

**As a** candidate,  
**I want** to create and manage my profile (personal details, skills, education, work history) with an onboarding checklist that tracks my completion progress and explicit privacy consent recording,  
**So that** my profile is ready to apply with and I have full visibility into what data is stored about me.

## Business Value

A complete, verified profile improves AI screening accuracy. The onboarding checklist reduces drop-off by showing candidates exactly what remains. Explicit consent recording ensures GDPR compliance from the first data collection point.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: Onboarding checklist tracks completion
**Given** a newly verified candidate visits their profile for the first time  
**When** they view the dashboard  
**Then** a checklist shows 5 items (basic info, skills, education, work history, privacy consent) each with unchecked status; profile completion percentage is 0%.

### Scenario 2: Profile update persists and checklist item completes
**Given** a candidate fills in their work history section  
**When** they save  
**Then** the data persists, the "Work History" checklist item is marked complete, and the completion percentage updates.

### Scenario 3: Privacy consent recorded with timestamp and version
**Given** a candidate accepts the privacy policy  
**When** they click "Accept"  
**Then** the acceptance is stored with `candidate_id`, `policy_version`, `accepted_at` timestamp, and `ip_address` in the database.

### Scenario 4: Profile edit is full CRUD
**Given** a candidate has an existing profile  
**When** they edit their email, delete a skill, and add a new education entry  
**Then** all three changes are saved independently and the profile reflects the updated state on reload.

### Scenario 5: Profile completion gate for applications
**Given** a candidate's profile completion is below 80%  
**When** they attempt to submit an application  
**Then** a prompt directs them to complete their profile first; they cannot proceed until the threshold is met.
