# US-003 — No-Show Rate Tracking and Weekly Analytics Digest Email

## User Story

**As a** recruiting manager,  
**I want** to see interview no-show rates and 30-day trend sparklines on the analytics dashboard and receive an automated weekly digest email every Monday at 08:00 with the prior week's KPI summary,  
**So that** I am informed of attendance issues and receive regular performance snapshots without visiting the platform.

## Business Value

No-show rate visibility identifies systemic scheduling or communication gaps. A proactive weekly digest eliminates the need for managers to build manual reports and ensures KPI awareness even during busy periods.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: No-show rate computed and displayed
**Given** 20 interviews were scheduled last week and 4 were marked "no_show"  
**When** the analytics page loads  
**Then** the no-show rate is shown as "20% (4 of 20)" alongside a 30-day trend sparkline.

### Scenario 2: 30-day trend sparkline updates daily
**Given** the analytics page is loaded on different days  
**When** the trend sparkline renders  
**Then** it shows the daily no-show rate for the past 30 calendar days as a line graph.

### Scenario 3: Weekly digest email sent every Monday at 08:00
**Given** the Monday digest job is configured  
**When** the BullMQ cron job fires at 08:00 Monday (platform timezone)  
**Then** each recruiting manager receives an email with the prior week's KPI summary (applications, shortlist rate, time-to-hire, no-show rate, offer acceptance rate).

### Scenario 4: Digest email skipped if no activity in the week
**Given** no applications were submitted in the prior week  
**When** the digest job fires  
**Then** no digest email is sent and the job logs "No activity — digest skipped."

## INVEST Checklist
