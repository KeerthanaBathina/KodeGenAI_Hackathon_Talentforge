# US-001 — Browse and Filter Open Job Requisitions with Pagination

## User Story

**As a** candidate,  
**I want** to browse open job requisitions and filter them by department, location, employment type, and experience level with keyword search,  
**So that** I can quickly find relevant roles without scrolling through dozens of unrelated listings.

## Business Value

Relevant role discovery reduces candidate frustration and increases qualified application rates. Pagination prevents performance degradation as the requisition list grows.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: Filter by department returns matching roles
**Given** requisitions exist across Engineering, Marketing, and Finance  
**When** a candidate selects the "Engineering" department filter  
**Then** only Engineering requisitions are shown and the filter chip is visible above the results.

### Scenario 2: Keyword search finds matching roles
**Given** requisitions exist with titles "Senior Backend Engineer" and "Marketing Analyst"  
**When** the candidate types "backend"  
**Then** only "Senior Backend Engineer" appears in results; typing clears previous results; debounce is 300 ms.

### Scenario 3: Pagination loads correct page
**Given** 50 open requisitions exist  
**When** the candidate navigates to page 2 (page size 20)  
**Then** requisitions 21–40 are shown and the URL includes `?page=2`.

### Scenario 4: No results state displayed gracefully
**Given** no requisitions match the active filters  
**When** the filtered list is empty  
**Then** an illustration and "No open positions match your filters — try adjusting your search" message is shown; the "Clear Filters" button resets to the full list.
